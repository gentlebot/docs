## What do we do?

We are a **[design and development agency](https://resynced.design/about)** specializing in making responsive and gorgeous applications both for our clients and for the open-source community. This documentation will go over some of our templates and help you set up your projects.

## Templates

Our templates are available in various formats to ensure seamless integration into your preferred stack. Whether you're creating a **landing page**, a complex web application, or a community-enhancing **Discord bot**, we’ve got you covered.

- **Websites & Landing Pages**  
  Includes coded versions in **[HTML](https://resynced.design/templates/html)**, **[Next.js](https://nextjs.org/)**, and **[Nuxt.js](https://nuxt.com/)**, alongside **[Figma](https://www.figma.com/)** source files. Customize layouts, colors, and fonts effortlessly to fit your branding.

- **Discord Bots**  
  Ready-to-deploy templates for versatile Discord bots, coded in **[Node.js](https://nodejs.org/)** and **[TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/)**, with customizable commands and integrations. Elevate your community experience with engaging, user-friendly bots.

- **Backend Projects**  
  Templates for scalable **[Go](https://go.dev/)** and **[Node.js](https://nodejs.org/)** backends, including API scaffolding, authentication flows, and deployment guides.
